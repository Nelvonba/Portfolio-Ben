Creating a logo for this particular client was challenging because they were reluctant to change the name of their company, which was both lengthy and common.

To work around this, I decided to write the name directly and highlight the letters "N" and "P" in a different color to create visual interest. As the client shared their goal of opening a new clinic, I also kept in mind the limited space available for signage and focused on making the logo as visible as possible from a distance.
