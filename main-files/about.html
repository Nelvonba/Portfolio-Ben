1/Biography


For the past 7 years, I've been working in the dental field, collaborating with more than 4 big clinics to enhance their online and offline marketing strategies.

While I thoroughly enjoyed my time in the dental industry, I've decided to follow my heart and pursue my passion for web development. There's just something about this fantastic world that keeps me hooked. I'm excited to dive deeper and I believe that the key to success in this field is to stay curious, adaptable, and passionate about what you do. And that's exactly what I aim to do, now and in the years to come.

Born and raised in France I live now in Montreal. I'm helping business owners with my skills using HTML/CSS, Sass, Javascript and React framework to create websites and web content to increase their visibilty (SEO oriented) and creating more organic trafic. I love meeting new people and working with them.
